## What it is

A done-for-you cold-email service for independent restaurants — we
write the sequences, handle deliverability, and book tasting
appointments with local corporate buyers.

## Why I'm building it

Restaurants have the product and the margin but not the outbound
muscle. My background is in DTC growth and automation; the two
collide here in an unfair way.

## Where it is

Live with four paying clients as of April 2026. Writing about the
build publicly as I go — linked posts below.

📋 𝗗𝗘𝗦𝗖𝗥𝗜𝗣𝗧𝗜𝗢𝗡
If you've heard of Onlyfans, this was a similar concept before Onlyfans.  B2B2C SaaS custom content creation platform. We achieved product market fit, however, we failed to scale due to relying on ads to promote the consumer-facing experience.  As Onlyfans was gaining momentum we shut down instead of pivoting and competing.

• Innovated with a lean team of 5, without taking excess capital or scaling prematurely.
