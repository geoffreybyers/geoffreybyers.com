## What it is

We started the company with the hypothesis that consumers wanted a healthy vaping alternative.  This company is a DTC e-commerce product startup focused on bringing the world's first Vitamin B12 vaporizer to market.  I grew and scaled the company from concept through to cash flow positive.  The business exists today without my daily involvement.

## Why I'm building it

Restaurants have the product and the margin but not the outbound
muscle. My background is in DTC growth and automation; the two
collide here in an unfair way.

## Where it is

Live with four paying clients as of April 2026. Writing about the
build publicly as I go — linked posts below.
