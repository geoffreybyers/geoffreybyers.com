First week of live outbound. 240 contacts across two buying centers.
Two replies, one booked tasting.

Lessons:

1. Subject lines referencing the buyer's specific venue beat generic ones 4x.
2. Thursday 7:00 AM local beat every other send time we tested.
3. Two follow-ups is the sweet spot before a restaurant goes silent.
